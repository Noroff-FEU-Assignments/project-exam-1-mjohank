Mastering the art of Espresso demands precision. Start with high-quality, finely ground coffee beans and ensure a consistent, fine grind. Tamp the coffee grounds firmly, creating a compact puck. Extract the espresso under high pressure, producing a small but potent shot. The result is a liquid concentration of coffee perfection.
